Convulsions (Battlerage)                      Known: Yes
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Syntax:            STARE <target> CONVULSIONS
Works on/against:  Denizens
Cooldown:          16.00 seconds
Resource:          14 rage
Details:

Cooldown:          23.00 seconds
Resource:          25 rage
